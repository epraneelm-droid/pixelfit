FAQ

Is my photo uploaded to any server?

No. All processing happens in your browser. Your image never leaves your device.

What if the file is too large?

Hit "Auto-Compress to Fit" — it reduces quality step-by-step until the file meets the limit.

What formats are supported?

Upload JPG, PNG, or WebP. Choose your preferred output format at download time.

Works on mobile?

Yes — mobile-first design. Works on any Android or iPhone.

Can I enter custom dimensions?

Click "Custom Size" and enter any width, height, and max KB you need.
